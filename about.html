About Us

“Education is our passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to the people who prepare for it today.”

The roots of education are bitter, but the fruit is sweet.

We thrive on our Tradition of Excellence, since 1995 we have always believed Excellence not only in academics, but the overall development is paramount in a child to make the Future of India Bright.

  • Our school provides education in more understandable way.
  • We are provided more practical exposure in learning so that it is easy to learn.
  • Our school follows strict rules in discipline and timing and hence we are punctual in our work.

Finally, we have friendly teachers and staff members in our school.
